Table-Top RPG Carousing Table

Instructions:

It's time to see how much carousing your characters are able to enjoy at the tavern! Press the Roll d20 button below. If the roll is 10 or higher, your player successfully handled their drink and may enjoy another, or stop carousing. If they continue to drink, press the Roll d20 button again to continue (up to a maximum of 5 drinks). If they choose to stop, press the Success button, and read the results of their night of carousing. If they fail at any time, press the Failure button, and read their results. The more drinks the players enjoy, the more disastrous the results of their carousiing could be, but if they succeed, the rewards are also much greater!
